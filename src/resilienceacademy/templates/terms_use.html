Goals

CRD is an open access web-based platform, launched by the Resilience Academy Initiative of the World Bank in January 2018. Designed to be continuously updated, this platform aims to become a reference for resilience-related decision makers, University communities and stakeholders on the Urban resilience.

Definitions

  1. “Platform” or “CRD” is an open access web-based platform launched by the Resilience Academy Initiative of The World Bank in January 2018. It consists of three main components providing:
